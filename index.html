About Brahma Kumaris

Worldwide Spiritual Movement

Brahma Kumaris is a worldwide spiritual movement dedicated to personal transformation and world renewal. Founded in 1937, we now have thousands of centers across more than 100 countries.

Our teachings focus on meditation, self-awareness, and spiritual understanding to help individuals cultivate inner peace and contribute to a better world.

"The quality of our thoughts determines the quality of our life."

Benefits of Raja Yoga Meditation

Mental Clarity

Experience improved focus, reduced stress, and greater mental peace through regular meditation practice.

Emotional Balance

Develop emotional stability and cultivate positive relationships with yourself and others.

Spiritual Growth

Deepen your connection with the divine and discover your true spiritual identity.
